Website Hosting

This website is hosted via GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc. When you visit this website, technical access data such as your IP address, browser type, requested page, date, and time may be processed by GitHub to deliver the website securely and reliably.

The legal basis for this processing is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. The legitimate interest is the secure and reliable operation of this website.

Cookies and Analytics

This website does not use analytics, advertising, tracking cookies, contact forms, newsletter forms, or payment systems. The website itself does not set cookies.

Fonts and Local Assets

Fonts and visual assets are loaded locally or through system fonts. The website does not load Google Fonts or other external font services.

External Links

This website links to external services, especially GitHub. If you follow those links, the privacy policies of the respective external providers apply.

Email Contact

If you contact the operator by email, the information you provide will be processed to respond to your request. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR and, where the request concerns a contractual or pre-contractual matter, Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

Boozle Application

According to the current website information, the Boozle application does not collect telemetry and does not transmit user PDFs to the website operator. It runs locally on the user's device.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you may have the right to request access to your personal data, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection to processing. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection supervisory authority.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated when the website changes, for example if analytics, forms, third-party embeds, or paid services are added.
